## 1. Concept Definition
The **Regenerative Bio-Medical Blockchain Relay (RBBR)** is a specialized communication protocol designed to bridge high-integrity medical data with decentralized ledger technology. It acts as the secure "handshake" between regenerative therapeutic logic and sovereign financial/data systems.

## 2. Technical Architecture
The Relay operates as a middle-ware layer that ensures medical advancements—such as the **ArthroRegen Nanoshot**—are deployed with immutable provenance.

* **Data Integrity:** Validates that therapeutic schematics remain unaltered during transmission.
* **Decentralized Relay:** Utilizes a node-based architecture to prevent single points of failure in critical medical infrastructure.
* **Smart Contract Triggering:** Can automatically execute "Proof of Treatment" or "Proof of Research" milestones on-chain.
